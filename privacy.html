Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how JustLines collects, uses, and protects your information, especially in relation to TikTok integration.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide (such as your TikTok username, videos you generate, and captions) and technical data (such as browser type and usage statistics). When you connect your TikTok account, we access your basic profile information and upload videos on your behalf, as permitted by TikTok's API scopes.

2. How We Use Information

We use your information to provide and improve our services, personalize your experience, and communicate with you. TikTok data is used only for the purposes of video upload and profile display, and is not shared with third parties except as required to operate the service or by law.

3. Sharing Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with service providers as needed to operate JustLines, or as required by law. TikTok data is handled according to TikTok's API policies and is not stored longer than necessary.

4. Data Security

We use reasonable measures to protect your data, including encryption and access controls. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Choices

You may request deletion of your data or opt out of certain features by contacting us at support@justlines.app. You can revoke TikTok access at any time via your TikTok account settings.

6. Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use of JustLines means you accept the new policy. We will notify users of material changes via the website or email.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at niquwrld@outlook.com.
